Case Study

Haya Makeover —
From Broken Site to Booked Out.

A luxury home-salon brand in Lucknow needed a website that could actually take bookings. Here's what we changed, and why it mattered.

The Project

Luxury Home Salon,
Lucknow, India.

Haya Makeover's old site was running on WordPress and quietly losing them business — broken navigation, no mobile booking flow, and a contact form that didn't reliably deliver enquiries.

We rebuilt it from the ground up as a fast, static site: redesigned navigation, JS-driven animations that actually run smoothly on mobile, and a booking flow rewired to land straight in WhatsApp — where their clients were already messaging anyway.

Fixed navigation and scroll bugs across all 7 pages
Migrated booking & contact forms to WhatsApp-based submission
Added SEO schema, meta descriptions, and legacy URL redirects
Rebuilt service cards to link directly into category-specific booking flows
